Musical instruments are devices that create sound, enriching melodies across various cultures and traditions.
From the deep tones of a cello to the bright notes of a xylophone, instruments exist in many forms, each offering distinct sounds and rhythms. 
Whether they are strings, winds, percussion, or electronic, these instruments enable musicians to convey creativity and emotion through the shared language of music.
